Abstract

We present a novel approach for learning steering behaviors in autonomous systems through data-driven methods and advanced control strategies.

Our method leverages machine learning techniques to understand complex steering dynamics and enables autonomous vehicles to navigate challenging environments with human-like precision. By combining reinforcement learning with traditional control theory, we create a robust framework that can adapt to various driving conditions and scenarios.

We demonstrate that Learn to Steer significantly improves navigation performance across diverse environments, from urban streets to off-road terrain. Our approach addresses key challenges in autonomous steering, including uncertainty in sensor data, dynamic obstacle avoidance, and real-time decision making.
